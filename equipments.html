Equipments Used

Equipment used in energy audits is essential for assessing energy performance and identifying efficiency opportunities. These instruments are crucial for accurate analysis and recommendations.
Some of the equipments we uses are:

Clamp Meter

It is an electrical test tool that combines a basic digital multimeter with a current sensor.It is safe as no wire is touched.

Digital Thermometer

They measure temp. across a range of industrial & clinical environments. These work best for fragile objects.

Multimeter

It is a test tool used to measure two or more electrical values - (voltage ,currents and resistance).

PAC Meter

PAC(Programmable Automation Controller) meter offers multi-discipline control

Sound Level Tester

It has a microphone to measure the changes in air pressure produced by the air source.

Tachometer

Tachometer is electromechanical device that output a voltage proportional to their shaft speed.

Combustion Analyzer

This instrument has in-build chemical cells which measure various gases suvh as CO2,CO,NOx,SOx etc.

Fuel Efficiency Monitor

This measures Oxygen and temperature of the flue gas,based on which it calculates the combustion efficiency.

Flow Meter

Flow meter is used to measure fluid flow in a pipe or open channel. It can be used for water, gases, chemicals and other fluids.
